About Us
Who we are
We are here to make your life easier and happier. Fashscape was born out of the love for fashion and is here to help you in your everyday life. You feel like you don't have anything to wear? Here is the place that can change that. You can look through the products that other users are selling and you will find something to match your aesthetics for sure. You feel that your wardrobe isn't big enough to support all your items, or you feel like you gathered so many clothes that you no longer wear? Here is also the place that you 'can get rid of them', by selling and showing them off to the whole world.
Our Mission
Our mission is, of course, to make your life happier by helping you in the journey of fashion.
